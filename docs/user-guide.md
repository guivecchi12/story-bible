# Story Bible — User Guide

Story Bible is a writer's companion app for organizing the world-building elements of your stories — characters, factions, locations, plot events, and more — across multiple books and writing teams.

---

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Navigate to the Story Bible web app.
2. Click **Register** on the login page.
3. Enter your name, email address, and a password.
4. Click **Create Account**. You will be redirected to your dashboard.

### Logging In

1. Enter your email and password on the login page.
2. Click **Sign In**.
3. To log out, click **Sign Out** in the top-right navigation bar.

---

## Books

A **Book** is the top-level container for all your story content. You can create and manage multiple books.

### Creating a Book

1. Click the book dropdown at the top of the left sidebar (shows your current book name).
2. Click **+ New Book**.
3. Type a name and press **Add**.

### Switching Books

1. Click the book dropdown in the sidebar.
2. Select the book you want to work in. All content in the sidebar will update to reflect that book.

### Renaming a Book

1. Open the book dropdown.
2. Hover over the book name — a pencil icon will appear.
3. Click the pencil icon, edit the name, and click the checkmark to save.

### Deleting a Book

1. Open the book dropdown.
2. Hover over the book and click the trash icon.
3. Confirm deletion. **This action is permanent and cannot be undone.**

> Only the book **owner** can rename or delete a book.

---

## Content Sections

All content is scoped to your currently active book. Use the left sidebar to navigate between sections.

| Section     | What it tracks                    |
| ----------- | --------------------------------- |
| Characters  | People and beings in your story   |
| Powers      | Abilities, magic systems, skills  |
| Motivations | Character goals and drives        |
| Factions    | Groups, organizations, alliances  |
| Locations   | Places, settings, maps            |
| Story Arcs  | Major narrative threads           |
| Plot Events | Individual events with dates      |
| Timeline    | Chronological view of plot events |
| Items       | Objects, artifacts, weapons       |

### Adding an Entry

1. Navigate to the relevant section (e.g. Characters).
2. Click **+ Add** or **New Character** (button label varies by section).
3. Fill in the fields and click **Save**.

### Editing an Entry

1. Click on an existing entry to open it.
2. Edit the fields and save.

### Deleting an Entry

1. Open the entry.
2. Click **Delete** and confirm.

## Collaboration

### Inviting a Collaborator

1. Navigate to **Settings** (visible in the sidebar if you are the book owner).
2. Enter the collaborator's email address and select a role (**owner** or **collaborator**).
3. Click **Send Invitation**. The invitee will receive an email with an acceptance link.
4. Invitations expire after **7 days**.

### Accepting an Invitation

1. Open the invitation email and click **Accept Invitation**.
2. If you already have an account, log in and you will be added to the book.
3. If you don't have an account, register first and then use the link.

---

## Reports

1. Navigate to **Reports** in the sidebar.
2. Select the type of report you want to generate.
3. Click **Generate Report** to download a PDF.

Reports include a title, date-time stamp, and tabular data for the selected content.

---

## Settings (Owners Only)

The **Settings** page is visible only to book owners. From here you can:

- View and manage current book members.
- Send and revoke invitations.
- Edit book details (name, description).
